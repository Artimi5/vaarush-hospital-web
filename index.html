The Heart Of Healthcare

A 15 Bedded well equipped hospital with separate Male & Female General Ward, Highly Intensive Care Unit, A/C Deluxe Rooms, A/c Twin sharing Room, Major & Minor Operation Theatre Founded in 2022, Vaarush Hospital is a private Multispeciality hospital. One of the best healthcare center in Mumbai having state-of-the-therapeutic and intensive care facilities. Located in Nallasopara West. Staffed by the finest medical and administrative personnel in Nallasopara, the hospital renders highly specialized services in areas with E.C.G. Orthopedic, Nephrology, Neurology, Diabetology, Gasteroentrerology, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Urology, General & Laparosopic surgeries are done. We offer our patients efficient medical services for a better healthcare treatment. 24x7 Pharmacy offering medicine. Health Check-up Packages. All Types of Pathology Test including Special-test are done in Best Affordable Packages.
